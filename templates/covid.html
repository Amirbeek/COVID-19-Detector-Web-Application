Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the COVID-19 Detector web application developed as a final year project at Brunel University. The application is designed to demonstrate the capabilities of AI-driven diagnostics without storing any personal data or medical images.

No Data Storage: We do not collect or store any personal identification information or medical images. Users can interact with the application by uploading images for detection purposes, but these images are processed in real-time and are not saved on any servers or databases.

Usage of Information: Any data entered into or generated by the application is used solely for the purpose of demonstrating the technology during the session and is not recorded or used beyond the scope of this project.

Data Protection: We adhere to strict guidelines to ensure that all data used during operation is protected and only accessible during the active session. There is no retention of information once the session is terminated.

Changes to This Privacy Policy: This policy may be updated or changed as necessary to reflect feedback and project development. Users are encouraged to frequently visit this section to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information that we process.

Contact Us: If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this application, please contact us via our contact page.
